## Overview

Midday Components is a collection of open-source components extracted from Midday, a business management platform. These components are production-tested and designed for real-world SaaS applications.

## Features

- **Production Tested** – Components used in a live SaaS product
- **Business Focused** – Designed for business applications
- **Modern Design** – Clean, professional aesthetics
- **Open Source** – Free to use in your projects
- **shadcn/ui Based** – Built on shadcn/ui foundation

## Usage

Browse the Midday components and copy the ones you need into your project.
