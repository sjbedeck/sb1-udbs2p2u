Achromatic is a production-ready Next.js 16 SaaS starter kit that includes everything you need to build and ship your SaaS faster. Save countless hours of development time with a complete foundation that's ready for production.

## Features

- **Authentication**: Full implementation with email/password, social sign-in (Google, Microsoft), MFA/2FA, account linking, and session management
- **Billing & Payments**: Stripe integration with billing portal, subscription management, and invoice handling
- **Multi-Tenancy**: Built-in organization support with member management, invitations, roles, and permissions
- **AI Chatbot**: Streaming chat UI with AI SDK integration and credits system for usage-based billing
- **Credits System**: Usage-based billing with credit balances and top-ups
- **Database**: Choose between Prisma or Drizzle ORM starter kits
- **File Storage**: Drag & drop uploads with S3/R2 presigned URLs
- **Marketing Pages**: Complete landing page with pricing, blog, documentation, and legal pages
- **Mailer & Templates**: Beautiful email templates for transactional emails
- **Analytics**: Event tracking and user behavior analytics
- **Monitoring**: Error tracking and application monitoring
- **Documentation**: Built-in docs with Fumadocs
- **Mobile Friendly**: Responsive design optimized for mobile devices
- **Dark Mode**: System-aware theming with smooth light/dark transitions
- **SEO-Ready**: Meta tags, sitemaps, structured data, and social images
- **AI-Optimized**: Custom rules for Cursor, Claude Code, and Gemini
