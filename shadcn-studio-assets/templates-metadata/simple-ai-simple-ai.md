## Overview

Simple AI provides a collection of components and blocks designed specifically for building AI-powered applications. It offers ready-to-use UI elements that make it easy to create chat interfaces, AI assistants, and other AI-driven features.

## Features

- **AI-Focused Components** – Pre-built components designed for AI applications
- **Easy Integration** – Simple to add to existing projects
- **Customizable** – Fully customizable to match your brand
- **Built on shadcn/ui** – Leverages the power of shadcn/ui components
- **Open Source** – Free to use and modify

## Usage

Browse the Simple AI component library and copy the components you need directly into your project.
